Who Are We?

Activism Always is a woman & minority-led company.

Activism Always aims at empowering mission-driven organizations through AI-driven data insights and strategy recommendations. Responding to a lack of data management talent in the social impact and nonprofit sectors, Activism Always provides a hybrid service to support.

Activism Always manages a group of programs including consulting services, the Social Media Strategy Clinic for Small Business as well as the Activism Always Product.

The Activism Always Product is the core of Activism Always's technology and is designed to be user-friendly for all organizations with no training necessary.
